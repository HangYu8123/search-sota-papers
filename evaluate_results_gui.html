3 · Quality rubric

Dimensions from ScholarQABench and DeepResearch Bench RACE. A run can be broad and shallow, or deep and off-brief — one satisfaction score cannot tell them apart.

4 · Verifiable checks

Binary and ground-truthable — no rater drift. Leave any as skip and it drops out of the reward entirely rather than counting against the run.

Localizes the failure. Bulk-set, then correct the exceptions.
