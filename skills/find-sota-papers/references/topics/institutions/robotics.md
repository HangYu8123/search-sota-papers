# Topic key: `robotics`

**Matches** robot learning, manipulation, locomotion, humanoids, embodied AI,
navigation/SLAM, field robotics, and robot foundation models.

**Applied in addition to `general.md`, never instead of it.** Read this file only
when the user's `topics` match the key above; a robotics run's accept-list is the
147 general entries **plus** the 10 below.

These are organizations whose robotics output is strong enough to matter for a
robotics search but that do not rank into the general list on overall AI volume —
which is exactly why a general-purpose list under-serves a robotics query. None
of them duplicates a general-list entry.

| Institution | Country/Region | Robotics focus |
|---|---|---|
| Physical Intelligence (π) | USA | Robot foundation models / generalist manipulation policies |
| Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT) | Italy | Legged and humanoid robots, compliant actuation |
| DLR — Institute of Robotics and Mechatronics | Germany | Torque-controlled manipulation, space and medical robotics |
| Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) — H²T | Germany | Humanoids, grasping and manipulation datasets |
| KTH Royal Institute of Technology — RPL | Sweden | Robot learning, planning, human-robot interaction |
| University of Bonn | Germany | LiDAR odometry/SLAM, agricultural and humanoid robotics |
| Honda Research Institute | Japan / USA | Humanoid locomotion, human-robot interaction |
| NAVER LABS (incl. NAVER LABS Europe) | South Korea / France | Service robotics, visual localization and mapping |

| Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI) | China | Embodied AI, task-and-motion reasoning |
