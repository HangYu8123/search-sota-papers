# Topic key: `medical`

**Matches** medical and healthcare AI, clinical foundation models, clinical LLMs,
medical imaging, computational pathology and radiology, and electronic-health-record
modeling.

**Applied in addition to `general.md`, never instead of it.** Read this file only
when the user's `topics` match the key above; a medical-AI run's accept-list is
the 147 general entries **plus** the 9 below.

These are hospitals, cancer centers, and clinical-AI groups whose medical-imaging
and clinical-model output is strong enough to matter for a healthcare search but
that do not rank into the general list on overall AI volume. None duplicates a
general-list entry (the broad-excellence band already carries the medical
universities — Harvard, UCSF, Johns Hopkins, Karolinska, NIH — so those are not
repeated here; the entries below are dedicated care-and-imaging institutions).
