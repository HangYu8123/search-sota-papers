# Topic key: `recsys`

**Matches** recommender systems, information retrieval (IR), generative retrieval
/ semantic IDs, learning-to-rank, neural ranking, and retrieval-augmented
recommendation.

**Applied in addition to `general.md`, never instead of it.** Read this file only
when the user's `topics` match the key above; a recsys/IR run's accept-list is the
147 general entries **plus** the 7 below.

These are recommendation-and-retrieval specialists — consumer platforms and IR
research groups — whose output is strong enough to matter for such a search but
that do not rank into the general list on overall AI volume. None duplicates a
general-list entry (the large industry labs — Google, Meta, Amazon, Alibaba,
ByteDance, Tencent — are already accepted, so their recommender work counts
without appearing here).

| Institution | Country/Region | Recsys / IR focus |
|---|---|---|
| Netflix | USA | Large-scale recommendation and ranking |
| Spotify Research | Sweden / USA | Music and podcast recommendation; sequential modeling |
| Criteo AI Lab | France | Computational advertising and recommendation |
| Pinterest | USA | Visual discovery and graph-based recommendation |
| University of Massachusetts Amherst (CIIR) | USA | Retrieval models and question answering |
| University of Glasgow (Terrier IR group) | UK | Neural ranking and retrieval |
| RMIT University | Australia | Retrieval evaluation and test collections |
