# Topic key: `autonomous-driving`

**Matches** autonomous driving, self-driving, robotaxis, driving world models,
end-to-end driving, perception and prediction for driving, and driver-assistance
(ADAS).

**Applied in addition to `general.md`, never instead of it.** Read this file only
when the user's `topics` match the key above; an autonomous-driving run's
accept-list is the 147 general entries **plus** the 10 below.

These are self-driving companies and automotive research labs whose driving output
is strong enough to matter for such a search but that do not rank into the general
list on overall AI volume. None duplicates a general-list entry (the general
industry labs — NVIDIA, Google, Baidu, Amazon — are already accepted, so their
driving programs count without appearing here).
